Logistics

Travel to Park City: Park City is about a 45 minute drive from the Salt Lake City Airport. There are public transportation, but Josh Levine has started a Google Doc to collect everyone's travel info in hope of finding some way to car pool. Here's the (link).
Food: There are restaurants everywhere in Park City.
Rooming Situation: We are split between two houses with 4 bedrooms each. Depending on the number of people who show up, we might need to share rooms.
Cost: VIS will be helping out with the housing expense (and possibly some amount on food). So you just need to pay for your way to get to Park City!
